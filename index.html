OUR SERVICES

At Valet NYC, we provide an extensive range of amenities and convenient valet services exclusively for residents in upscale residential buildings. We're committed to ensuring a seamless experience, leaving no aspect to chance, and promptly addressing the needs of our valued patrons with efficiency.


VALET SERVICES

Residents can request the following services:

  • Dry Cleaning
  • Shirts Laundered
  • Tailoring
  • Wash and Fold Laundry
  • Leather and Suede Cleaning
  • Shoe Repair and Shoe Shine
  • And many more customized services to meet your needs

HOUSEKEEPING

Our housekeeping and maid services include, but not limited to:

  • All Floors Swept and Mopped
  • Rugs Vacuumed
  • Beds Made
  • Rooms and Frames Dusted
  • Bathroom Cleaned (includes floors, toilets, sinks and tubs/showers)
  • Kitchen Cleaned (includes microwave, stove top, and countertops)
  • And many more

COMMERCIAL SERVICES

Landlords and Property Managers, feel free to contact us for:

  • In-house Residential Services
  • Pick-up & Drop-off Laundry Services such as Doorman Uniforms, Lab Coats, Towels, etc.
  • Package Room Management, Handling, and Receiving
  • Post Construction Cleaning
